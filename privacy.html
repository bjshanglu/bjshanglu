Global Privacy & Data Governance

Enterprise Compliance Framework | Version 3.0 | Last Updated: October 25, 2023

1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY & LEGAL ENTITY

This Global Privacy Policy (the "Policy") is issued by Shanglu (Beijing) Technology Co., Ltd. (hereinafter "the Company", "we", "us", or "our"). As a specialized technical team providing mobile application and mobile game services globally, we recognize that data privacy is a fundamental human right. This Policy outlines our "Privacy-by-Design" architecture, ensuring compliance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA), the PRC Personal Information Protection Law (PIPL), and the strict developer policies of Apple Inc. and Google LLC.

2. GRANULAR DATA TAXONOMY: WHAT WE COLLECT

We classify the information we process into specific categories to ensure maximum transparency:

2.1. PERSISTENT IDENTIFIERS & TELEMETRY

To provide ad attribution and maintain service continuity, our system automatically captures:

  • Advertising Identifiers: IDFA (Apple), AAID (Google Play), GAID, and IDFV.
  • Network Metadata: IP Address (used for approximate geolocation at a city level), BSSID, SSID, and MAC addresses (obfuscated as per modern OS protocols).
  • Device Blueprint: Manufacturer, hardware model, OS version, kernel version, battery level, carrier, and signal strength.

2.2. BEHAVIORAL & ECONOMY LOGS

To optimize gameplay and monetization through IAP and subscriptions:

  • Engagement Path: Level completion times, mission failure rates, tutorial abandonment, and feature interaction heatmaps.
  • Virtual Economy: Balance of virtual currency, transaction IDs for in-app purchases, and consumption patterns of premium features.
  • Stability Metrics: Crash dump files, memory usage logs, and network latency samples.

4. CROSS-BORDER DATA FLOWS & SOVEREIGNTY

Your data may be processed on servers located outside your jurisdiction. EEA Users: We utilize Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission. PRC Users: Data collected within China is localized on domestic servers. Cross-border transfers for global game synchronization are performed only after strict internal security assessments and, where necessary, CAC-level reporting.

5. NEUTRAL AGE MANAGEMENT & CHILD SAFETY

COPPA & GDPR-K PROTECTIONS

Our Services implement a **Neutral Age Screen** upon launch. If a user identifies as a minor under the age of digital consent (e.g., 13 in the USA, 16 in certain EU nations, 14 in China):

  • Zero-Tracking Mode: All behavioral identifiers (IDFA/GAID) are permanently revoked.
  • Filtered Monetization: We only serve non-targeted, age-appropriate contextual ads.
  • Community Lockdown: Chat and social features are disabled to prevent unauthorized contact.

7. TECHNICAL SECURITY INFRASTRUCTURE

Our R&D Center utilizes a Zero-Trust Architecture. Data at rest is encrypted using AES-256-GCM. Data in transit is protected via TLS 1.3. Our databases are isolated within Virtual Private Clouds (VPC) and are inaccessible from the public internet. We conduct quarterly automated penetration tests and bi-annual compliance audits.
